Who?
I'm a peruvian student recently graduated from high school last year. I studied lots of math practically all of my life, more than 10 years already. I thouht it was my passion until I found coding in 2021. Specifically, competitive programming. Working on algorithms to solve difficult problems is my day to day. Solving a problem and then getting "Accepted" is the best sensation that can ever exist. It's my drug, shortly. Some people ask me: "Angie, do you have a life? or is all about algorithms?". I usually say I do have a life. But writing algorithms is my life.
